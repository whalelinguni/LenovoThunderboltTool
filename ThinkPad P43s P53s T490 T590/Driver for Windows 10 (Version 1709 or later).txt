This package installs the software (Thunderbolt driver) to enable the following devices.

    Thunderbolt(TM) Controller-15BF

Supported Systems

    ThinkPad P43s (Machine Types: 20RH, 20RJ)
    ThinkPad P53s (Machine Types: 20N6, 20N7)
    ThinkPad T490 (Machine Types: 20N2, 20N3, 20RY, 20RX)
    ThinkPad T490 (Machine Types: 20Q9, 20QH)
    ThinkPad T590 (Machine Types: 20N4, 20N5)

Supported Operating Systems

    Microsoft Windows 10 64-bit (Version 1809 or later)
